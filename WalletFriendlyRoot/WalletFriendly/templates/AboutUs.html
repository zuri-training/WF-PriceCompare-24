ABOUT US

Every day at WalletFriendly, we help millions of shoppers on the web research, compare prices and buy online in a matter of clicks. Whether it’s the latest Adidas sneakers or the latest Samsung Galaxy, you will find it on WalletFriendly.com. In order to make the best decision, it’s good to have all the facts at your fingertips. We are one of the world’s best price and product comparison services, with one single goal – to guide consumers to smarter buying decisions.

Our range of services are designed to ensure optimum levels of convenience and customer satisfaction with the retail process; these services include our lowest price guarantee, dedicated customer service support and many other premium services.

Did you know that 8 out of 10 shoppers landing on WalletFriendly have done their online homework? And that over 73% of shoppers are more likely to buy from an unknown retailer if seen on WalletFriendly
