Frequently Asked Questions

Am I supposed to make any payment here?

No you shouldn't. WalletFriendly does not charge or take payments from our users. You can check the options for payments when you are checking out on the merchant's website (their payment options may vary)

How do you compare the prices for the product?

We display prices regardless of whether or not a retailer is a customer of WalletFriendly. Retailers are sorted with the lowest price excluding shipping at the top as standard and then in ascending order.

Do you sell and products?

No. Wallet Friendly is simply an information service. We help you find the best products at the lowest price. It is the retailers we link to that sell the products, not WalletFriendly. Please check the ratings other customers have given the retailer before you make a purchase.

How can WalletFriendly list so many retailers?

Wallet Friendly has a technical solution that matches the available products, retailers and prices, for both online retailers and some physical retailers. Some aspects of our data processing are automated, while other parts require manual work.

How are prices updated?

Most prices are automatically updated at least once a day. For a lot of retailers, prices are updated more often. So, double-check the price on the retailer's website before completing a purchase to ensure that the price you have seen on WalletFriendly is current and correct.
